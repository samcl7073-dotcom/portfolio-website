CMU Datathon 2026

February 15, 2026 •

Analyzing critical challenges in global aid distribution.

Sourced directly from UN humanitarian teams. This Datathon tackles two core problems: Targeting Validation, building models to benchmark budget-to-beneficiary ratios and flag anomalies in project efficiency; and Geo-Insight Challenge, mapping the "funding gap" by identifying geographic regions where humanitarian needs outweigh current pooled fund coverage.
