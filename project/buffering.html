Short film: Buffering…Please Wait

June 15, 2023
1 / 8

Buffering…Please Wait is a science fiction drama short film directed and written by Samantha Cheryl Lai. It debuted in 2023 and has been shown at several international film festivals.

Overview

  • Title: Buffering…Please Wait
  • Director / Writer: Samantha Cheryl Lai
  • Genre: Sci-fi, drama
  • Runtime: ~13 minutes 17 seconds
  • Production: Canada (filmed in Taiwan)
  • Languages: English and Mandarin Chinese
  • Budget: Around $35,000 USD

Plot/Theme

The story follows a nameless woman who explores a memory device embedded in her brain in an attempt to rediscover a lost identity. As she delves deeper, she finds that the truth she uncovers has an emotional weight she struggles to bear. The narrative blends futuristic tech concepts with an introspective emotional core about memory and loss.

Festival Screenings & Awards

The short has been featured in a number of festivals worldwide:

  • Fantasia International Film Festival (Montreal) — Official Selection
  • Boston Science Fiction Film Festival — USA Premiere
  • The Philip K. Dick Science Fiction and Supernatural Festival — Won Best Philip K. Dick Short
  • Busan New Wave Short Film Festival (Korea) — Won Best Cinematography

About the Filmmaker

Samantha Cheryl Lai is a Canadian writer-director. Buffering…Please Wait is known as her narrative directorial debut, inspired in part by personal themes of loss and identity.
