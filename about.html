About Me

Hi, I'm Samantha. I'm a UX researcher and interactive systems designer with a background in narrative-driven media. Trained in the Master of Entertainment Technology program at Carnegie Mellon University, I approach interactive products as systems shaped by human behavior, motivation, and context.

My foundation in filmmaking and narrative design informs how I frame research questions, interpret user behavior, and communicate insights to cross-functional teams. I'm particularly interested in games and emerging technologies, where storytelling, system design, and user experience intersect at scale.
